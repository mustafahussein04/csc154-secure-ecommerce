Defense-in-depth demo

Every transaction here
is locked, verified, checked.

Coffer is a small storefront built to prove a point: registration, login, browsing, and checkout can run end-to-end without a single unverified byte in transit or at rest. Look at the strip above — that's not decoration, that's the actual session state, live.

TLS

Encrypted in transit

Every page is served over HTTPS via a locally-signed certificate authority — no exceptions, no plain HTTP fallback.

HASHED

Credentials at rest

Passwords are hashed before they ever touch the database. Nothing plaintext is stored, ever.

RBAC

Role isolation

Buyers, merchants, and admins are strictly separated — crossing that line gets you a 403, not a workaround.
